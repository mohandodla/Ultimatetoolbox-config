8. Children's Privacy

   

The App is rated for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App does not require registration and does not collect personal data from any user.

     

9. Data Sharing

   

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third party. The only data that leaves your device is handled by Google's advertising and billing services as described above.

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers or databases. When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is automatically deleted.
